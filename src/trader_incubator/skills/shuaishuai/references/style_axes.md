# Style Axes

Use combinations from different axes to keep trader styles distinct.

## Primary Axis
- trend-following
- mean-reversion
- value-investing
- event-driven
- macro-regime
- volatility-breakout

## Signal Horizon Axis
- ultra-short
- intraday
- swing
- multi-day

## Risk Axis
- low-turnover
- neutral-beta
- high-conviction
- strict-stop-loss

## Construction Hint
When you have N traders, avoid repeating the same primary axis unless necessary.
If repeated, separate by horizon and risk axis, and keep declared pairwise similarity <= 0.5.
